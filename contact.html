Our Story

Founded in 2005 by Robert Jenkins, a third-generation builder with a passion for craftsmanship, Summit Builders began as a small local contractor. Today, we've grown into a full-service construction firm serving commercial and residential clients across the region.

Our philosophy is simple: build with integrity, deliver on promises, and exceed expectations. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to create structures that stand the test of time.

What sets us apart is our commitment to sustainable building practices, innovative solutions, and maintaining the highest safety standards on every job site. Our team of certified professionals brings decades of collective experience to each project.
